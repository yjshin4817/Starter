What is the Ratatouille?

Ratatouille is a French Provençal stewed vegetable dish, originating in Nice, and sometimes referred to as ratatouille niçoise (French: [niswaz]).
Recipes and cooking times differ widely, but common ingredients include tomato, garlic, onion, courgette (zucchini), aubergine (eggplant), bell pepper,
and some combination of leafy green herbs common to the region.
Also, Ratatouille is the name of a famous movie.
